About Me

Hi, I am Kaushik Nandan Saha.

I am currently a Senior Administrative Assistant at Baeldung.com, a fully remote EdTech company based in Romania. In this role, I leverage project management software like Jira to effectively distribute tasks within our team, ensuring timely project completion. My responsibilities include managing the front-end aspects of our website and Teachable course platform, maintaining course pages on WordPress, and managing our website widgets. Additionally, I write SOPs, track report updates, and maintain effective communication with team members and our CEO. I also oversee social media announcements, ensuring our audience stays informed and engaged with the latest updates from Baeldung.com.

Since joining Baeldung in October 2022, I have utilized Google Suites, DRIP (a marketing automation platform), and Google Analytics to enhance our administrative processes. My work ensures that our online courses and website deliver an optimal user experience, from front-end maintenance to seamless course page management.

I have been deeply involved in Data Analysis since early 2021, driven by a passion for uncovering insights through data. These experiences have instilled in me a strong drive to learn new technologies. Currently, I am expanding my knowledge in Machine Learning techniques, particularly interested in Customer Behavior Analysis and Pattern Prediction on various datasets.

I am also proficient in SQL for database querying and Tableau for creating insightful visualizations and dashboards.

My work on various projects, including business case studies and environmental data analysis, demonstrates my capability to provide data-driven solutions. I enjoy the process of asking questions, exploring, cleaning, and analyzing datasets to uncover meaningful insights.

Customer Riding Habit

This Case Study is a part of GOOGLE Data Analytics Professional Certification. The Case study was done on a publically available dataset provided via Coursera. The case study looks into the Riding Habit of the customers of a fictional bike share company Cyclistic. The data analysis process includes the Riding habit of the Casual Riders and Annual Members of Cyclistics.

The analysis was done using "R". Sereal packages such as Tidyverse, DataExplorer, Lubridate was used. I looked into customers riding habit by different time of the day, different Season, Month. Casual Riders rides very differently than Annual member. This analysis could give valuable insights into the casual riders. Various Ad campaign could be launched to turn the casual riders to annual members.

London Bike Accident

This is an Exploratory Data Analysis Project done on Bike accident in London from 2015-2019. The dataset is part of MakeoverMonday (week 31) project. I used various R packages like: Tidyverse, SP, DataExplorer, rgdal, Lubridate. The SP package was used to map out the accident spot in the streets of London.

The analysis was done based on the severity of the accident like fatal, serious, and slight. The analysis points out the victims of the accidents. The analysis also shows which day's of the week or what time of the day was most prone to bike accident.

US Ecommerce Record EDA

Analysis done on US Ecommerce Record of 2020 dataset from Kaggle. In this analysis project I looked mostly the relation between sales and profit for each product sub-category. I used several R packages e.g. Tidyverse, geojsonio, rgdal, broom, mapproj, Lubridate.

I found out which product are most sold and which brings out most profit, whether there is a relation between discount, product sold, and profit. I ploted a hexmap using geojsonio, rgdal, broom which shows which state ordered the most items.
